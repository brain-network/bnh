NetSci 2018's Brain Network Hackathon.

BNH will be the first of its kind: a hackathon entirely dedicated to brain networks. If you've never heard of it, don't worry. Express tutorials will be given at the very beginning of the event.

Propose your project

You need more brains & hands to help you realize your project about brain networks? You are pretty certain your data has something to say but don't know how to make it talk? Join us as a Brain Net' Conqueror by filling in this form.

Share your kowledge

You don't have any specific project to work on, but think your expertise in complex networks, machine learning, neuroscience or else could help us. You're more than welcome to join us as a Brain Net' Hacker by filling in this form.
